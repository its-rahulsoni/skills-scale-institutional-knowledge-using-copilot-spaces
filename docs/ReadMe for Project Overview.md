Summary of New Content

Propose to add new personas/roles to project management process documentation, including clear descriptions of their responsibilities and interactions with existing roles. Suggested additions: UX Designer, Quality Assurance Engineer, Business Analyst, DevOps Engineer, and Support Lead. Each new persona will include:

Role summary
Key responsibilities
Typical communications and interactions
How they fit alongside current roles (Developers, Project Managers, Product Managers)
Why is this update needed?

Expanding the set of defined roles will clarify accountability and communication in projects, help new team members understand who does what, and ensure critical activities (e.g., design, support, analysis) are addressed in our processes. As we've grown, work has broadened across specialties that aren't well described in the current documentation. Clearly documenting new personas will:

Close gaps in process clarity
Reduce confusion and role overlap
Accelerate onboarding
Improve collaboration by making expectations and handoffs explicit

Additional Personas & Roles (Proposed)

UX Designer

Role Summary: Designs intuitive, user-centered solutions and advocates for end-user needs in all phases.

Responsibilities:

Create wireframes, prototypes, and mockups
Collaborate with stakeholders and devs on requirements
Conduct usability testing and feedback cycles
Ensure accessibility standards are upheld
Typical Communication:

Review sessions with Product Managers and Developers
Usability feedback sessions with users and stakeholders
Quality Assurance Engineer

Role Summary: Ensures delivered features meet quality standards and are free of critical defects.

Responsibilities:

Write and maintain test cases
Execute manual and automated testing
Collaborate with Developers on defect remediation
Verify acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
Typical Communication:

Daily standups
Bug triage meetings
Test planning with Developers and PMs
Business Analyst

Role Summary: Translates business needs and requirements into actionable items for the delivery team.

Responsibilities:

Gather and clarify requirements from business stakeholders
Document user stories and business process flows
Propose solutions and evaluate options with the team
Typical Communication:

Requirements workshops
Syncs with Product Managers and key business owners
DevOps Engineer

Role Summary: Builds and maintains infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and operational monitoring.

Responsibilities:

Configure CI/CD pipelines
Monitor applications in production
Coordinate deployments and rollback procedures
Typical Communication:

Coordination with Developers on releases
Incident response and status updates
Support Lead

Role Summary: Acts as primary point of contact for post-release support and user-reported issues.

Responsibilities:

Triage user support requests
Escalate issues to appropriate teams
Feedback insights from the support queue to project planning
Typical Communication:

Weekly syncs with Product Managers
User support and documentation updates
